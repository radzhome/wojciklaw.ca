The Lawyer

Greg Wojcik is the founder and the owner of the Law Office Wojcik Professional Corporation.

Greg is a distinguished alumnus of the University of Toronto, having earned a Bachelor's Degree in Commerce from the esteemed Rottman School of Management. Following the completion of his degree, Greg embarked on an enriching journey, relocating to Warsaw, Poland, where he dedicated a year to contributing his expertise in the Strategic Planning and Development Department of the Warsaw Transit Authority.

Upon his return to Toronto, Greg pursued his passion for law at Osgoode Hall Law School, a constituent college of York University, ultimately securing a Bachelor of Law degree. He honed his legal skills through articling at a reputable firm specializing in Real Estate and Family Law, and in 2008, Greg took a significant step by establishing his own practice.

Currently residing in Toronto with his wife Aneta, daughter Margaret, and their beloved Labrador retriever, Frankie, Greg's personal life is as vibrant as his professional pursuits. In his leisure moments, he immerses himself in the pages of history books, indulges in travel adventures, and passionately follows the triumphs of the Toronto Blue Jays, whether from the comfort of his home or cheering in person at the stadium.
